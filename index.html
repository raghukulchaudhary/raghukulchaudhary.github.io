A brief on who I am and what drives my work in regulated healthcare environments.

I am a Quality Assurance professional with over five years of hands-on experience across Medical Device and IVD sectors. My work spans the full spectrum of QMS activities — from complaint management and change control to CAPA, risk management, and regulatory compliance.

Currently at Vantive Healthcare, I manage customer complaints from South Asia using TrackWise, execute change control and NC workflows, and support internal audits. Previously at Nulife and Astam Diagnostics, I built QMS frameworks aligned to ISO 13485 and ISO 9001.

I am a Six Sigma Green Belt certified professional and bring a structured, data-driven approach to every quality challenge.
